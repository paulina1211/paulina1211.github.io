Kids Kit -

Intro to User Interface Design (CS 3750)

May 2020 - July 2020

This portfolio website outlines the research, analytic, and design processes of creating a solution to alleviate the struggles of preK and Kindergarten teachers and parents while teaching remotely due to COVID-19. Through our interview, survey, and ideation process, we decided that a craft kit, complete with instructions for parents would be the most creative, simple, and cost-effective solution that would fill our user’s needs!

I created this website for our team using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, and was the first website I ever coded myself!
